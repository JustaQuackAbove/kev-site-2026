Scene Page

Scroll to move one pinned square scene forward or backward.

The scene stays in place while the squares progress. Scroll down to advance it. Scroll up to reverse it. The motion finishes before the section ends so the final frame can hold for a bit.

Use data-scene-start-hold for the static start hold Use data-scene-end-hold for the static end hold

After The Scene

This is where normal page scrolling can continue.

Adding content after the pinned scene makes the prototype feel more like a real page. The scroll pauses to drive the square animation, then releases back into regular document flow once the scene is done.

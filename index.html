Square Motion Lab

Reusable click and scroll effects for any square.

Give a square a trigger class, add one or more effect classes, then tune it with small data attributes. 

<div class="motion-box animate-on-click effect-slide effect-grow"
  data-slide-x="120"
  data-scale="1.3"
  data-duration="450"></div>

Click Trigger

Click a square to toggle its motion.

Slide

animate-on-click effect-slide

Grow

animate-on-click effect-grow

Spin

animate-on-click effect-spin

Mix Effects

effect-slide effect-grow

Scroll Trigger

Scroll down and the squares animate when they enter view.

Slide On Scroll

animate-on-scroll effect-slide

Grow On Scroll

data-scale="1.45"

Spin On Scroll

data-rotation="360"

Run Once

data-scroll-once="true"
